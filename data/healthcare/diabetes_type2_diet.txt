Type 2 Diabetes & Diet (tip 2 diyabet)
Goals: HbA1c (<7% typical), fasting glucose 80–130 mg/dL, postprandial <180 mg/dL.
Diet: high-fiber vegetables, lean protein, whole grains; limit refined carbs and sugary drinks. Consider low‑GI foods.
Medication: metformin is common first-line unless contraindicated; add GLP‑1 RA or SGLT2 per comorbidities.
Monitoring: foot care, annual retinal exam, kidney function, BP and lipid control.
